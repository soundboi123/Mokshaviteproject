About us
Our Mission
To make bassheads happy and girl wet. Moksha stands for the release of the karmic cycle. The ending of rebirth by living to once's full potential.
Who We Are
We are Moksha Vibrations, freedom fighters for the souls and mind. We are group of naughty boys who love bass. Most of us met eachother in school, we've all had an artistic education.
Music Genres
We're just confused, we love many genres like Goa- & Psytrance, Reggea & Dub, Tekno & Hardcore. We do not discriminate, every artist in our collective has their interests and favorites. This way we have a very versitile way of thinking, we combine ideas from different genre's and put them in others.
